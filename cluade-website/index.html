How We Make It

From Seed to Bottle

Our cold press process preserves every bit of goodness that nature packed into each seed — no heat, no shortcuts.

1

Sourcing Seeds

We carefully source quality seeds from trusted local farmers across Tamil Nadu.

2

Cleaning & Sorting

Every batch is hand-cleaned and sorted to remove impurities before pressing.

3

Cold Press Extraction

Pressed at low temperatures using traditional wooden churners to preserve nutrients.

4

Natural Settling

Oil is allowed to settle naturally — no centrifuge, no bleaching, no deodorising.

5

Bottled Fresh

Packed in food-grade bottles and sealed to lock in freshness and aroma.
